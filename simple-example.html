Hello world!

Usage:

Slurp in and parse your Twitter timeline like this. Your data will be stored in #simpleTwitterFetcherData as stringified JSON in the data-simple-twitter-fetch attribute:

simpleTwitterFetcher.fetch('415143052894674946');

Retrieve data like this:

simpleTwitterFetcher.retreive();

Add a second parameter to specify how many tweets you want to parse and store locally.

simpleTwitterFetcher.fetch('415143052894674946', 1);

simpleTwitterFetcher.fetch('415143052894674946', 3);
